About the Group

Rooted in Trinidad.
Built for the Region.

Terran Resources is a privately held industrial group founded and operating in Trinidad and Tobago. We build, invest in and operate companies across sectors where precision, reliability and deep domain expertise create lasting competitive advantage.

Our portfolio spans engineering and manufacturing, quarrying and bulk materials, and enterprise technology — with each business run by specialists committed to delivering exceptional outcomes for clients, partners and communities across the Caribbean.

We take a long-term view: investing in people, infrastructure and innovation to ensure our companies are built to endure.
